Executive Takeaways

  • Hono/Bun is the best overall balance under brutal benchmark pressure.
  • Go remains the lightest in memory and strongest for minimal VPS sizing.
  • .NET 10 is stable and capable, but has the highest runtime footprint.
  • FastAPI is acceptable at low load, but tail latency grows sharply in soak.
If the question is “which stack eats the least resources while still surviving heavy tests?”, the answer is Go. If the question is “which stack gives the best overall balance of speed and resource use?”, the answer is Hono/Bun.
